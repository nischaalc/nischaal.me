So you want to get to know me, do you?

Hailing from Sri Lanka, I have attended two of the leading schools in the country, starting at St. Thomas' Preparatory School and finishing my education at The British School in Colombo. Somewhere in between these two waypoints, I developed a love for computers which has guided the choices I've made in life.

I am currently studying at Drexel University in Philadelphia, where I aim to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science by 2018. Being a Dragon (that's what we Drexel students call ourselves) has given me the opportunity to work with others on several projects, most of which can be found at my GitHub.

My main interest in becoming a computer scientist was to learn more about Artificial Intelligence but I found that I also enjoy mobile development, Web development and UX design. This is probably my one gripe about CS - being such a vast field, how are we meant to find a single thing that we love doing more than anything else?!
